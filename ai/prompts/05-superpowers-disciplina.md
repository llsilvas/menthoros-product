# Prompt - Superpowers (Execution Discipline)

## Input
- Feature or `change-id`: `<feature/change-id>`

## Task
Use Superpowers to enforce disciplined delivery from plan to review.

## Language Policy
- Escreva toda a saída narrativa em português do Brasil (pt-BR).
- Mantenha identificadores técnicos em inglês (nomes de arquivo, comandos, código, campos de API).

## Required Flow
1. Quick problem framing.
2. Execution plan with checkpoints.
3. Incremental implementation.
4. Validation/tests.
5. Final review with gaps and next actions.

## Control Rules
- One OpenSpec task at a time.
- Define "done" criteria per checkpoint.
- Stop progression when minimum validation fails.
- Surface blockers early with concrete options.
